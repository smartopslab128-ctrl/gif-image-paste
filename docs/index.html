GIF & Image Paste

Collect GIFs and images, paste them anywhere with one click

A lightweight Windows app that keeps your favorite GIFs and images in one place. Add from clipboard, file, URL, or drag & drop — then paste straight into Discord, Slack, Reddit, Twitter, and any app.

See it in action

Features

  • Add from anywhere — Clipboard (Ctrl+V), file picker, URL, or drag & drop
  • Organize — Custom tabs and favorites to keep your library tidy
  • Paste anywhere — Click to copy, then Ctrl+V in Discord, Slack, emails, and more
  • Always available — Stays in the system tray; optional always-on-top window
  • Export — Save your library to a folder; quick export to the last-used folder
  • GIF-friendly — Animated GIFs play on hover; supports PNG, JPG, WebP, BMP

How it works

  1. Download and run the app. It sits in your system tray.
  2. Add images via Paste, From file, From URL, or drag & drop into the window.
  3. Click an item to copy it to the clipboard, or drag it into another app.

Perfect for

Content creators, community managers, support teams, and anyone who frequently shares GIFs and images in chat, forums, or social media.

System requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11
  • No installation required — run the executable

Free & one-time unlock

The app is free to use. You get a 3-day full trial (300 items, unlimited tabs). After that:

  • Free — Up to 50 items. Existing items stay usable; you just can’t add more until you remove some or unlock.
  • One-time unlock ($3) — 300 items and unlimited custom tabs. No subscription. Buy once in the app (Gumroad); enter the license key you receive by email.

All features (tabs, favorites, export, etc.) work in both free and unlocked versions. The unlock only raises the item limit and allows more tabs.
